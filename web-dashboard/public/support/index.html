BCN Plant Scout Support

BCN Plant Scout is the Base Camp North field app for plant photos, GPS points, return notes, and synced scouting records.

For app help, sign-in issues, synced plant records, account access, or bug reports, email support@basecampnorthpa.com.

Account deletion

In the BCN Plant Scout app, open Account / Sync and choose Delete Account and Cloud Data. After confirmation, the app deletes your BCN Plant Scout account, cloud-synced plant records, and cloud photos.

The direct account deletion help page is scout.basecampnorthpa.com/delete-account.
