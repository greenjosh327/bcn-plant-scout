Delete Your BCN Plant Scout Account

BCN Plant Scout is an app from Base Camp North. Users can request deletion of their account and associated cloud data at any time.

How To Request Account Deletion

  1. Email Base Camp North with the subject line BCN Plant Scout Account Deletion.
  2. Include the email address used to sign in to BCN Plant Scout.
  3. Send the request through the Base Camp North contact page: https://basecampnorth-pa.square.site/contact-us

Data Deleted

After verifying the request, Base Camp North will delete:

  • The BCN Plant Scout account record.
  • Cloud-synced plant observation records.
  • Cloud-synced plant photos and photo metadata.
  • Associated GPS coordinates, notes, return dates, and collection data.

Data Kept

Local records stored only on the user's device cannot be deleted by Base Camp North. Users can delete local records directly in the app or remove the app from their device.

Base Camp North may retain limited records if required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup restoration. Backup copies, if present, may remain for up to 30 days before automatic deletion.

Timing

Account deletion requests are usually processed within 30 days after verification.
